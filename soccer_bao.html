Throughout the match, France controlled the ball for 66% of the time, attempted 31 shots, while Gibraltar failed to register a single shot in the entire 90 minutes.

However, the opening goal for the French national team came from an own goal by defender Ethan Santos in the 3rd minute. Just 1 minute later, Marcus Thuram scored, making it 2-0 for the Gallic roosters.

In the 16th minute, Zaire-Emery extended the lead to 3-0 for France, and shortly after, Gibraltar suffered a setback as defender Ethan Santos received a straight red card. By this point, Gibraltar's fighting spirit had officially crumbled, and Mbappe and his teammates from the French national team unleashed a goal-scoring spree.
